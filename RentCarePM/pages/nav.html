Our Story

Established in 2014, RentCare Property Management stands as a testament to innovation and unwavering dedication in the real estate landscape. What began as a bold venture has since blossomed into a cornerstone of excellence, propelling RentCare to the forefront of New Zealand's property management sphere. At the helm is General Manager and co-founder, Jason Whitty, whose sterling reputation in the Wellington property market laid the foundation for RentCare's meteoric rise. Joined by former optometrist Philip in 2015, whose financial acumen now steers the ship as Finance Manager, RentCare embodies a synergy of expertise and passion. Under Jason's astute leadership, RentCare strikes a harmonious balance between personalized service and the formidable capabilities of a market leader. Beyond mere transactions, we prioritize relationships, operating with the finesse of a boutique agency coupled with the robust infrastructure of an industry giant. As investors ourselves, we empathize with the challenges of property ownership. Thus, our mission transcends mere management; it's about alleviating your burdens and safeguarding your investments. With RentCare, rest assured that your property is not just managed, but nurtured with utmost care and professionalism.
